3. Interpreting Results

The final result is calculated based on your Strictness Mode setting.

  • GENUINE: The note passed the minimum required forensic points.
  • SUSPICIOUS: The system detected multiple physical or digital anomalies.
Pro Tip: Use a score of 12/20 for general use, and 16/20 for high-security verification.

4. Best Practices for Images

  • Lighting: Avoid direct flash. Use ambient, even lighting for best color shift analysis.
  • Alignment: Keep the banknote flat and straight. Avoid crumpling.
  • Resolution: High-resolution images (1080p+) are required for Microprint detection.
